BREAK THE RULES - WEB DESIGN (2019)

When studying design, there are often many guidelines and rules we are taught to abide by in order for our design to be successful.

This site aims to ask all the ‘What If’s of design and to test its boundaries. To depict this rulebreaking in its success, the site includes a curation of various designers and design groups who have experimentation in their work.

This can hopefully be encouragement for studying designers who have an unconventional style and are worried about becoming accepted in the field.
