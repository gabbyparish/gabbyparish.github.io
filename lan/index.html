If you're new to the world of design, excited to unleash your creativity but feel constrained by the conventions of design, WELCOME!

This site contains a collection of designers doing it big in the industry in their own way, rather than doing what is expected of them.

It can take some time to fully flesh out your own style (even by the time you graduate!) so in making this site, there is hopefully some inspiration for all the possibilities in design.

REMEMBER: THE BEST WAY TO STAND OUT AS A DESIGNER IS TO JUST BE YOU AND EXPERIMENT ALL THE TIME
